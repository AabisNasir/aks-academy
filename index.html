FAQ

Questions, answered.

A few things people often ask before getting started. If your question is not here, reach out on WhatsApp.

Are classes online or onsite?

Both. I run live interactive online classes as well as onsite practical training and hands-on scanning sessions, so you can choose what fits your schedule and learning needs.

Which exams do you prepare students for?

I prepare candidates for the ARDMS Sonography Principles and Instrumentation (SPI) exam and the Obstetrics and Gynecology registry exam, with mock tests and performance assessment throughout.

Do you offer one-to-one mentorship?

Yes. One-to-one mentorship and examination coaching are available across all education programs, alongside weekly MCQ sessions and case-based discussions.

What scanning services are available?

Services include fertility and follicular tracking, early pregnancy, NT and anomaly scans, pelvic and TVS, abdominal, breast, thyroid, and prostate scans, plus second-opinion review.

Can I get a second opinion or remote review?

Yes. I offer second-opinion ultrasound review and tele-ultrasound consultation, which is useful if you already have images and want an experienced assessment.

Who are your programs for?

Sonographers, radiographers, gynecologists, fertility specialists, and healthcare students who want to develop or strengthen their ultrasound skills and exam readiness.
